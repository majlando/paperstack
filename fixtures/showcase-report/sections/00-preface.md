# Preface

This report documents the design and implementation of **Kantina**, a
pre-order system for the canteen at Example Academy, developed as the exam
project for the Software Engineering and Architecture (SEA) course.

The project was carried out by a group of three students over four sprints
between February and May 2026. All three group members contributed to every
chapter; the analysis, code, and measurements are the group's own work unless
a source is cited.

## Reading guide

Chapters 1–3 motivate the project and establish the process and requirements.
Chapters 4–7 cover the technical core: architecture, implementation, testing,
and concurrency. Chapters 8–9 evaluate the result. The glossary explains
domain terms, and the appendices contain the installation guide, the test
protocol, and the sprint backlogs.

Citations use APA author-date references; figures and tables are captioned and
numbered per chapter.
